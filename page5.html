Good-bye


On a table lies a man, for what reason did he die?
In the hospital for exploratory surgery

He was put to sleep A silent death.
His heart was tired It could no longer beat
He died.

They searched for an answer, looking for a cause.
Cancer of the lung , Cancer of the brain.
How had he lived this long?

I never got to say good-bye
In Memory of Raymond E Miller Sr.
 
By Barbara Ross



He Gave Us Life


From the darkness A glob appeared.
Bringing light from the heavens
Light to the world
And a dream became real.

People, rulers under God.
 Keepers of our world.

Water, oceans of water.
Home for fish, food for man.
Light for man to work by
Darkness for man to rest by.

A Garden fruit provided by God.
A place to live provided wisdom.
Life, Gods gift to man.

Everything was clean then came sin.

By Barbara Ross
