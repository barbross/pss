Words



I lost a Good friend today
Someone I've never met but friends we were.
Sometimes things just go wrong,

No intentions meant, feelings are hurt
Anger shows words start bouncing.
That's all we had

Words.

That makes it to easy to go separate ways.
But the feelings are still there.
The memories of moments shared.
Even if they were only Words.

The words meant a lot
I had a friend, I lost a friend

Due to Words

By Barbara Ross
