I Need You to Love Me

Don't know why you allowed that to happen to me.
I would have been good and loved you.

Made Mothers Days Cards for you.
Brought you flowers.

Helped daddy work on cars.

I would have grown up and made you proud.
Never made the choice you made

My child would have been your
grandchild.
The one you'll never have.

 I'll wait for you.


Some day I hope to meet you.
Give you those hugs and kisses that I missed.

I need you to, hold me and hug me

I need you to love me.

By Barbara Ross
